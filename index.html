Technical Program Leadership

Acceleration Path: Scrum Master / L3 → L4 → L5 → L6

Tier-1 Ready | GenAI Native | Promotion-Focused • 12 Weeks

Designed for professionals with 8–20 years of experience transitioning to Technical Program Manager or seeking level progression in Tier-1 companies (Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft). Master technical credibility, ambiguity navigation, cross-org influence, and interview readiness.

Core Curriculum (12 Weeks)

Week 1: TPM Role Clarity – From ceremonies to system-level ownership.
Week 2: Technical Depth – Architecture literacy and distributed systems basics.
Week 3: Ownership Shift (L3→L4) – Problem definition and structured clarity.
Week 4: Cross-Team Execution (L4→L5) – Dependency mapping and alignment.
Week 5: Roadmap Structuring – Sequencing initiatives under constraints.
Week 6: GenAI for TPMs – AI-driven documentation and risk synthesis.
Week 7: Business Framing – Translating technical work into business impact.
Week 8: Influence Without Authority – Managing senior stakeholder conflict.
Week 9: Ambiguity Navigation – Pre-mortem thinking and risk modeling.
Week 10: Scaling & Launch – Multi-region rollout planning.
Week 11: L6 Thinking – Strategy definition and scope expansion.
Week 12: TPM Simulation – Cross-org executive presentation.
